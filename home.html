Welcome to Sree MuthuKumaraswamy College:

Sree Muthukumaraswamy Devasthanam Educational Society was founded in 1978 with a view to providing man making, character building, value based education to the rising generation of boys and girls. The first step in the pilgrimage towards realization of the ideal was the starting of Sree muthukumaraswamy Devasthanam P.Venugopal Chetty primary school, Purasawalkam, in 1978. This was followed by Sree Muthukumaraswamy Devasthanam Hindu Primary School, park Town in 1979, Kandakottam Nursery & Primary School, Purasawalkam in 1980, Sree Muthukumarswamy Devasthanam Hindu Higher Secondary School, Park town in 1986, P.Venugopal Chetty Higher Secondary School, Purasawalkam in 1989 and Sree Muthukumaraswamy Devasthanam Kannapiran padmavathi primary school in 1990. The growth of the education endeavour shows steady and sure progress, and it is no mean achievement to have grown in less than two decades from a more handful of pupils and a couple of teachers to a scholastic dimension encompassing a wide area of North Chennai serving the academic needs of more than 3500 boys and girls. The team of dedicated teachers in 150 strong
